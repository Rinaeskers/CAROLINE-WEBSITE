About Us

Nyaika hotel combines a perfect blend of country side setting and city life. Perched at the top of the hilly town of fort portal in an attractive, well contoured and manicured tropical gardens, Nyaika is perfect for both the business and leisure traveler with modern amenities which will ensure that your meetings and events are effortless and successful, whilst our leisure activities and commensurate service and ambience ensures that our guests experience the very best of Nyaika hospitality. At an elevation of 2,500 ft located on Nyaika street less than 3kms southwest of the CBD, you will be able to catch a beautiful view of the Ruwenzori mountains, the iconic palace of the Toro nestled at the highest point in town and a magical 360 angle of the striking greenery as well as fort portal. Managed by Hallmark Hospitality Group ltd. the hotel which is the biggest in fort portal is strategically located and offers easy access to different attractions in the neighboring areas with striking sceneries, amazing nature and adventures. This include beautiful crater lakes, the chimps of Kibale forest and hot springs in Semuliki park.
